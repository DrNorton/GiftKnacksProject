KnacksGifter is designed to make the life more interesting, kind and full of respect by connecting people around the globe and creating meaningful sharing community.
If you love to help people, share and make them happier, if you have the opportunity to travel and want to meet people all over the world and bring joy to their life. Let people know about it here by leaving a message. From now on you will be called a Giver and receive many thanks. Thank you for your help!
If you miss something from your hometown or just want to explore the traditions of different countries, receive national souvenirs and try unusual food. Or you may not even know what you want and be just craving for new experiences – simply request it and other members will respond and help you. You'll be called a Wisher. Thank you for sharing your dreams with the community!
Meaningful sharing community fosters respectful communication and an atmosphere of mutual support and assistance. We believe that it is unconditional sharing that brings more light and kindness into our life, and opens up an opportunity to meet sincere and caring people around the world. When you offer your help and hand over a desired gift to someone, you, in return, may be taken to see the sights of the city, invited for a lunch or a tea, given a ride to a hotel etc. The way you arrange it is up to you. Our service provides all the communication tools you may need. As a Wisher remember to treat others as you would like others to treat yourself.
